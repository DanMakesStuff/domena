Introduction
Does it ever feel like Minecraft no longer offers anything new? Is everything starting to feel bogged down and lifeless during lockdown?

We faced the same issue.
Thats why Voice SMP was created! One of the first Minecraft servers with voice chat inspired by a popular twitch series Epic SMP, which we were very interested in and we would like to bring its unique features to ordinary players.

To keep the comunity friendly and united, we use whitelist.
(Also, you don't need to own the bought game to play here)
In order to play with us you'll need to notify some member of our team (On any mentioned socials). We will gladly welcome you to our comunity as long as you act reasonably (While still having fun, ofcourse).
Mods
At this time the server offers just two mods, but we're planning to add more later.

  • Create

    Create Mod is a sophisticated technical mod that alows players to move entire buildings, make interesting farms and much more.

  • Mumble

    Mumble mod is for proximity/voice chat, the main server feature.
How to join
Unfortunately, due to the complexity of Mumble mod, the necessary software installation is more demanding unlike the conventional servers.
We have prepared a tutorial for you on how to set everything up.
No worries, in 10 to 20 minutes you will be able to play on the server with others!

Files needed:

  • Forge installer 1.16.5 here - Minecraft version that supports mods.
  • Create 1.16.5 here
  • Mumble link 1.16.5 here - Mod for linking Mumble program with minecraft.

  • Mumble here - Standalone program responsible for voice chat.
