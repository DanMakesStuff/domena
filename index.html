About Me

3D Modelling

I first tried 3d modelling about 5 years ago and since then I've used Blender, Blockbench, Cinema 4D, Mine-imator or Sketchup in which I've made several small projects.

Graphics

Most of my experience in computer graphics comes from working on pixel art projects, but I've tried several other things too!

Programs used to make all of my work were Adobe Photoshop or Zoner photo studio. One of the first things I made were game related textures, profile pictures and desktop wallpapers.

Web Design

Web design. Although it's more time consuming and, in my opinion, most complex branch out these three, I find it very entertaining as it combines both programming and graphics design.

I was taught how to make websites on a web builder app in elementary school, but I only learned how to make actual websites about two years ago. Since then I've used VS Code.
